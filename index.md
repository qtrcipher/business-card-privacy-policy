# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Business Card iOS app, published by GCC Apps.

## Overview

Business Card is designed to help you create and share a digital business card. We aim to collect as little information as possible and to keep your card data under your control.

## Information You Provide

The app may let you enter business card details such as your name, job title, company, phone number, email address, website, social links, profile photo, logo, and other information you choose to include on your card.

You decide what information to add and what information to share.

## How Your Information Is Used

Your card information is used to:

- Create and display your digital business card.
- Generate sharing formats such as QR codes, links, or contact cards.
- Help recipients save your contact information when you choose to share it.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not email your contacts. We do not use your card information for advertising.

## Data Storage

Card information may be stored on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled in the app or by the operating system, Apple may sync your app data across devices signed in to your Apple ID. Apple’s handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple’s privacy policy.

## Sharing Your Card

When you share your card, the information you selected for that card may become visible to the recipient. Only share information that you are comfortable making available to others.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use Apple platform services such as iCloud, Contacts, Share Sheet, Wallet, widgets, or other iOS features. Those services are provided by Apple and are subject to Apple’s privacy practices.

If future versions add third-party services, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what data is used and why.

## Contacts

If the app requests access to Contacts, it will be used only for features you initiate, such as saving or preparing contact information. We do not upload your address book to our servers.

## Analytics and Tracking

Business Card does not track you across apps or websites. We do not use your information to build advertising profiles.

If future versions include privacy-preserving analytics, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or when those features are released.

## Children’s Privacy

Business Card is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will help address the issue.

## Data Deletion

You can delete card information from within the app, where supported, or by deleting the app from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, you may also need to manage or delete related iCloud data through Apple’s device or iCloud settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above.
